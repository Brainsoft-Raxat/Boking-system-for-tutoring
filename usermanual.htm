Introduction

Welcome my dear friend! This guide was created to help you to learn how to use the system. This guide contains step-by-step guidance on how to perform certain tasks, which will be described in chapters. We will also provide with a detailed explanation and images.

Main part:

Levels of access to the system: The availability of certain functions depends on the level of access. The system has 3 main levels of access:

  1. Student. You have access to chapters: 1, 2, 4, 5, 13
  2. Tutor. You have access to chapters: 1, 3, 4, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 13
  3. Administrator. You have access to chapters: 5, 12

Chapter 1.First meeting with the website

The first page that you will see when you open the website is index.php, which contains a number of titles accompanied with images and links to other pages. On the top side of the main page you will see the navigation bar, which is used for navigation between the pages. Each page of this website has this navigation bar.

On the bottom side you will see the footer, which also contains useful links and contact details of our educational center.

    If you are accessing website using device with small screen size, you will see such hidden navigation bar. If you want to activate the links, just press the “Hamburger” button.

If you will press the “Blog” button, you will see the list of articles authored by educational center. In order to read the article you want, just press the box where your desired article is included.

There is also “Available Courses” link, which shows the list of available courses offered by our tutors. 

You can access the “Contact Form”, which allows you to write message to administrator in case if you have some questions or recommendations.

    There you need to fill two boxes. In the first one you need to write your correct email, so the administrator will be able to send you the answer to your email. In the second box you need to leave your message. Be sure that the email is correct and the message contains more than 10 symbols.

Chapter 2.Registration of student

    On the navigation bar press the “register” button and choose the “register as a student” tab, after which you will be redirected to registration form. There you need to fill your personal information. Data you need to enter: username, first name, last name, email, date of birth, gender, grade, phone number and password.

Requirements for successful registration:

  1. All boxes are filled and “register” buttons is submitted
  2. Your username should be typed in English(latin keyboard).
  3. First name and Last name can be typed in Russian or English.
  4. Email should be valid.
  5. Passwords from two boxes should be the same.

If you followed these rules, you will get a message about successful registration. After that, you can login with the new account.

Chapter 3.Registration of tutor

On the navigation bar press the “register” button and choose the “register as a tutor” tab, after which you will be redirected to registration form. There you need to fill your personal information. Data you need to enter: username, first name, last name, email, date of birth, gender, qualification, subject, price per one lesson, phone number, address, years of experience, your avatar image and password.

Requirements for successful registration:

  1. All boxes are filled and “register” buttons is submitted
  2. Your username should be typed in English(latin keyboard).
  3. First name and Last name can be typed in Russian or English.
  4. Email should be valid.
  5. Uploaded image is in the right format. Acceptable formats: JPEG, JPG, PNG.
  6. Passwords from two boxes should be the same.

If you followed these rules, you will get a message about successful registration. After that, you can login with the new account.

Chapter 4.Login of students and tutors

    After login you will be able to use additional functions of the system. Here are the instruction how you can login.

  1. If you are student, first of all choose the appropriate user type, in your case is “student”. Then fill the boxes with your username and password and press the “Login” button. 
  2. If you are tutor, first of all choose the appropriate user type, in your case is “tutor”. Then fill the boxes with your username and password and press the “Login” button. 

Chapter 5. Administrator login

In order to use functions of admin panel, first of all you need to login as an admin. On the footer find the link called “admin”, then you will be redirected to admin login page.

    Here you need to insert your username and password that I have provided to you. Then press the “Login” button. 

Chapter 6. Student account management.

    Student account has less amount of functionality. You are only allowed to book lessons and delete bookings. 

To use the booking function you first of all need to be logged in as a student, which was mentioned in chapter 4. After which, open the page with “Available courses”, then find the the course you want. You also can use the filters on the left side of this page. You can choose the specific subject and price range. When you find an appropriate courses press the “book the lesson” button, after which, you will be redirected to booking form.

    At this stage, you will notice that some boxes are already filled and all you need is just to fill in the gaps with appropriate data. Choose the date when you want to have the lesson and select one of the available time slots. Then you may write details about your concerns. Then, press the “book” button. “Appointment booked successfully”  would mean that your booked new lesson.

   

    After successful procedure, press the button with your name on the top right side of the navigation bar to see your booked lessons. 

    There you can see your bookings. In case you want to cancel the lesson press the “[X]” button and confirm your action. Following this, message about successful delete will appear.

 

Chapter 7.Tutor account management: Update personal details

    By clicking the “Update details” button, you will be able to change information about you. You will see that boxes are filled with a data that you have filled when you have registered. In case if you want to change some details, choose one of the appropriate input boxes and insert the right data type. By pressing the “Choose file” button you can change your avatar. Then the image crop form will appear. 

Crop the image, then press “Crop & Upload Image” button. Following this, you will see that your image has been uploaded. In order to make changes the “Update” button should be pressed. Then you will see the “data updated successfully” message, which means that your changes has been applied.

 

Chapter 8. Tutor account management: Setting opening hours

    After you logged in as a tutor, in order to set your available hours press the “Set the time” link in the dropdown button with your name. Type your start and end time, and duration of one lesson in minutes. By doing this, you set the time period of your work, which then will be divided into time slots.  

    After successful registration, you can view your new schedule in “Schedule” link.

Chapter 9. Tutor account management: Using personal schedule.

    First of all, be sure that you logged in as a tutor. Then, press the “Schedule” link in the dropdown menu with your name. You will see the schedule that you have created before(Read Chapter 7). In case if you want to hide some time slots in your schedule in order to make them inaccessible for bookings, choose the right checkboxes and press “Hide” button. You also can make hidden lessons available by pressing “make them available” button.

Chapter 10. Tutor account management: Working with bookings

    At this stage you need to be logged in as a tutor. By pressing “Booked lessons” link in the dropdown menu with your name, you will be able to see the data about bookings that were made by students. In case you want to cancel the booking, press the “[X]” button and confirm your actions. 

Chapter 11. Tutor account management: Viewing statistics by periods

By pressing the highlighted links on the top, you will be able to view diagrams, which show day wise, month wise, year wise statistics of your bookings.

Chapter 13. Log out from the system.

     If you want to logout you need to press “logout” button on the top right side of the page. After which, your session will be deleted and you will be redirected to main page.

Chapter 15. Backup the files of the website

    In order to backup files of the website, login into beget and on the control panel choose the “Backup” tab. There you will see the files of your website, and you need to put a tick near the “ag_education_demo” and press the “Load backup copy” link.

Chapter 16. Recovery from Beget

    In order to recover previously backed up files, choose the date of last backup near the label “To select copy”. Then put a tick near the files you want to recover and press the recovery blue button.
